Understanding Keyloggers

A keylogger is a surveillance tool that records keystrokes on a device. While they have legitimate uses in parental control and corporate monitoring, malicious keyloggers are a significant cybersecurity threat.

Types of Keyloggers:

Hardware Keyloggers

Physical devices connected between keyboard and computer

Software Keyloggers

Programs installed on the target system

Wireless Keyloggers

Intercept wireless keyboard signals

Browser-Based

JavaScript-based logging in web browsers
